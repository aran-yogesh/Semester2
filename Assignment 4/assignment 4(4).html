import pandas as pd 
import seaborn as sns
import matplotlib.pyplot as plt
In[2]:
tips = sns.load_dataset("tips")

plt.figure(figsize=(8, 5))

sns.lineplot(
    data=tips, 
    x="total_bill",
    y="tip", 
    hue="time", 
    markers=True, 
    palette="muted", 
)

plt.title("Total Bill vs. Tip Amount (Lunch vs. Dinner Trends)")

plt.xlabel("Total Bill ($)")
plt.ylabel("Tip Amount ($)")
plt.show()

This sns lineplot visualizes the relationship between total bill and tip amount, comparing lunch vs. dinner trends using different colors. The lines highlight variations in tipping behavior across meal times.
